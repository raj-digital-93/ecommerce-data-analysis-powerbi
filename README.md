# Project Overview

This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to uncover sales, profit, and quantity trends across categories, products, customers, and time.
The goal is to help business stakeholders identify top performers, track monthly performance, and make data-driven decisions.

The dashboard is built using Power BI, with a focus on clear KPIs, drill-downs, and comparative insights.

# Business Objectives

-> Identify top-performing categories and products

-> Analyze customer contribution to revenue

-> Track month-wise trends for Sales, Profit, and Quantity

-> Compare performance across multiple business metrics

-> Enable faster decision-making through visual storytelling

# Dashboard Structure

The report is divided into 3 dedicated pages, each focused on a key business metrics.

1. Sales Performance Page

Insights covered:

-> Top category by Sales

-> Top 10 products by Sales

-> Top 5 customers by Sales

-> Month-wise Sales trend

Business questions answered:

-> Which categories and products drive the most revenue?

-> Who are the most valuable customers?

-> Are sales growing or declining month over month?

2. Profit Performance Page

Insights covered:

-> Top category by Profit

-> Top 10 products by Profit

-> Month-wise Profit trend

Business questions answered:

-> Which categories are actually profitable?

-> Are high-sales products also high-profit?

-> How does profit fluctuate over time?

3.  Quantity Sold Page

Insights covered:

-> Top category by Quantity Sold

-> Top 10 products by Quantity Sold

-> Month-wise Quantity trend

Business questions answered:

-> Which products sell in high volume?

-> Are high-quantity products low-margin?

-> How demand changes across months

# Tools & Technologies Used

-> Power BI

-> Power Query (data cleaning & transformation)

-> Data Modeling (relationships across tables)

-> Data Visualization best practices


# Sales Insights

-> Fashion is the highest-contributing category in terms of total sales.

-> T-Shirts are the top-selling product, followed by Watches and Running Shoes, indicating strong demand within the Fashion category.

-> November records the highest sales, followed by May and June, suggesting seasonal buying patterns.

-> Approximately 90% of total sales originated from the web channel, highlighting the importance of digital platforms in revenue generation.


# Profit Insights

-> The Fashion category also leads in overall profit, aligning with its strong sales performance.

-> T-Shirts generate the highest profit, followed by Watches and Running Shoes, indicating that top-selling products are also high-margin contributors.

-> November shows the highest profit levels, with May and June following, mirroring the sales trend and confirming seasonal profitability.


# Quantity Sold Insights

-> The Fashion category records the highest quantity sold, indicating strong demand and higher purchase frequency.

-> Watches lead in quantity sold, followed by Formal Shoes and Running Shoes, highlighting strong volume movement within the Fashion category.

-> November shows the highest quantity sold, with May and June following, reinforcing the presence of seasonal demand patterns.
